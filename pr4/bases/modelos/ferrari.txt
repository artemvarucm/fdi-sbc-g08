The Ferrari 488 GTB is a sports car that is distinguished by its 3.9-liter biturbo V8 engine that produces 670 horsepower. It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.0 seconds. Its consumption is approximately 11.4 L/100 km, which makes it a bit more efficient than other Ferraris with larger displacement engines. The 488 GTB is renowned for its exceptional aerodynamics and handling, providing a top-notch driving experience on any type of road.

The Ferrari LaFerrari, Ferrari’s flagship hybrid hypercar, combines a 6.3-liter V12 engine with an electric motor to produce a total output of 950 horsepower. It can go from 0 to 100 km/h in under 3 seconds and has a top speed of over 350 km/h. LaFerrari’s stunning design and groundbreaking hybrid technology make it one of the most advanced and revered Ferraris ever built.

The Ferrari 812 Superfast is powered by a 6.5-liter V12 engine that delivers 800 horsepower, making it one of the most powerful production Ferraris ever created. The 812 Superfast boasts a top speed of 340 km/h and can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.9 seconds. This car offers an unparalleled blend of performance, luxury, and driving excitement, establishing itself as a true grand tourer.
