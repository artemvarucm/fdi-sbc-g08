The Mercedes-AMG GT is a high-performance sports car powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, delivering up to 577 horsepower in the GT R variant. It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.6 seconds, with a top speed of 318 km/h. The AMG GT combines stunning design, exceptional performance, and state-of-the-art technology, making it one of the most exciting vehicles in the luxury sports car market.
