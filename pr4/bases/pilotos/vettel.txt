Sebastian Vettel, born on July 3, 1987, in Heppenheim, Germany, is one of the most successful drivers in Formula 1 history. Vettel, a four-time World Champion (2010-2013), joined Aston Martin (then Racing Point) in 2021. Throughout his career, Vettel has been known for his consistency, intelligent racecraft, and ability to extract the maximum from his car. His move to Aston Martin marked a new challenge after years with Red Bull Racing, and despite not winning titles with Aston Martin, he brought valuable experience to the team.

Titles and Achievements:
4 World Championships (2010, 2011, 2012, 2013)
53 Wins
57 Pole Positions
122 Podiums
