Michael Schumacher is widely regarded as one of the greatest Formula 1 drivers of all time, with an incredible 7 World Championships to his name, won in 1994, 1995, 2000, 2001, 2002, 2003, and 2004. Schumacher achieved 91 race wins, a record in Formula 1 until it was surpassed by Lewis Hamilton in 2020. He also held the record for 68 pole positions at the time and secured 155 podium finishes throughout his career. Outside of F1, Schumacher excelled in other motorsport competitions, including 4 wins at the Le Mans 24 Hours, further showcasing his versatility and skill across different racing disciplines.
