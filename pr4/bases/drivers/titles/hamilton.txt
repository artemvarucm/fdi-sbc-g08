Lewis Hamilton is one of the most successful drivers in Formula 1 history, having won 1 World Championship with McLaren in 2008. As of 2021, he has accumulated 103 race wins, 101 pole positions, and 176 podium finishes. Hamilton is also known for his dominance at the British Grand Prix, having won the race multiple times throughout his career. His achievements have solidified his legacy as one of the sport's greatest drivers.
