The Ferrari 812 Superfast is a front-engined grand tourer boasting a 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12 engine that generates an impressive 789 horsepower. It sprints from 0 to 100 km/h in 2.9 seconds and achieves a top speed of 340 km/h.

This model holds the distinction of being Ferrari's most powerful naturally aspirated V12 ever made for a production car. It also introduced electric power steering, a first in Ferrari’s history, enhancing precision handling. The car’s elegant design incorporates hidden air vents and active flaps, ensuring optimal aerodynamics and downforce.
