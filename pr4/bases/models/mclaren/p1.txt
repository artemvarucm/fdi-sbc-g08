The McLaren P1 is a hybrid hypercar that combines a 3.8-liter twin-turbo V8 engine with an electric motor, producing a total of 903 horsepower. It can go from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.8 seconds and has a top speed of around 350 km/h. The P1 is known for its advanced hybrid technology, aerodynamics, and driving dynamics, making it one of the most prestigious hypercars in the world.
