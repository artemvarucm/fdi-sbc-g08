The McLaren Artura is a plug-in hybrid supercar powered by a 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 engine combined with an electric motor, generating a total output of 671 horsepower. It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.0 seconds and has a top speed of 330 km/h. The Artura is the first model to be built on McLaren’s new Carbon Lightweight Architecture (MCLA), ensuring a lightweight yet incredibly rigid structure.

McLaren’s innovative E-Drive allows the car to drive short distances in full electric mode, making it one of the few supercars that offers a balance of performance and environmental consciousness. This model represents McLaren’s bold step into the future of hybrid supercars, showcasing advanced technologies while maintaining the brand's core focus on speed and driving dynamics.
