The Aston Martin Vantage is a sleek and stylish sports car powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, delivering 503 horsepower. It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.6 seconds and has a top speed of 314 km/h. The Vantage is known for its incredible handling, striking design, and thrilling driving experience, offering the perfect blend of power and sophistication.
