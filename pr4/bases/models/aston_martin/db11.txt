The Aston Martin DB11 is a grand tourer powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine or a 5.2-liter twin-turbo V12 engine, producing up to 630 horsepower in the V12 variant. It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.9 seconds and has a top speed of 322 km/h. The DB11 combines luxury with performance, offering exceptional driving dynamics and a truly refined experience, making it one of the brand's most desirable models.
